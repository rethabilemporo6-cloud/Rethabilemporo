Event overview

Maletsunyane Braai festival is an event that occurs in Lesotho, in the month of November anually. It takes place at the Maletsunyane falls, which is about 115km from the capital city of Maseru. This festival combines food lovers, adventurersand culture enthusiasts to celebrate Basotho culture and cuisine.
